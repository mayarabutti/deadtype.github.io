Looke is a video streaming application.

Looke

Looke is a video streaming application that works with TVOD and SVOD and allows users to watch movies, series and shows whenever they want and wherever they are. The content can be accessed by paying a monthly fee or through an one-time purchase. I'm currently in charge of developing the user interface and experience for all users on smart TVs, applications for iOS and Android and the website.

The application works on:

Smart TVs: Samsung and Philips.
