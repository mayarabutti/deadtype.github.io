Encripta

Encripta is a Brazilian company that provides solutions for managing and delivering media content. Moreover, they develop client-side applications for different platforms such as iOS, Android, desktops and web. I was in charge of brand development, layout and front-end user interface. Their website is available at http://www.encripta.com.br.
